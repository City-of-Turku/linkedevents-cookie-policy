User information and Cookies in the service Linked Events

This is the Cookie Policy of the service Linked Events. It can be found in the following address: https://linkedevents.turku.fi/

Collecting User Information

The web services of the City of Turku collect user information using cookies and corresponding technologies, such as tags in order to develop the services to correspond better to the needs of the service users. We cannot recognise a user only with the help of cookies or other technologies, and the services cannot recognise the email address of the service user unless the user has given that piece of information himself or herself.

When you sign in the service Linked Events, your email address and your name or nickname will be saved in the database. The latter information will be transferred in the context of the procedure of hearing, .e.g., to support the decision-making. The information will not be disclosed to other parties unless it has been mentioned in the context of hearing.

Types of Cookies

We use the cookies in the service Linked Events for anonymous user tracking. A cookie is a small text file which is stored on the device of the user (computer, smart telephone, tablet or other IT device), which

  • allows the moderator to recognize the devices / IP addresses and the visited contents and the used functionalities
  • makes logging in the websites easier, and
  • allows combining visitor information.

The service usability will be developed on the base of this data. The cookies will not be saved if you use the private mode of the browser. Neither will the login information be saved in the private mode of the browser.

We use the following cookies in the service Linked Events:

  • Strictly necessary cookies are cookies which are essential for the usage of the functions of the service Linked Events. They include, .e.g., the cookies which allow you to access the secure areas of our service.
  • Functionality cookies are used to identify you while you use our services. They allow us to remember your login information and your preferences (such as language). The information collected with these cookies may be anonymous and they may not track your browsing activities on other websites or digital services.
  • Analytical cookies and performance data cookies collect information on the usage of our services. We use the web analytics application Matomo Analytics, .e.g., to track how the clients land on our website and how they navigate on the page and use the website. It also helps us improve the performance of our websites and applications, e.g., by making sure that users can easily find what they are looking for on our website. The information stored by these cookies does not allow us to identify you.

Please find more general information of the cookies in an article HTTP Cookies in Wikipedia.

Information about the Cookies and the Corresponding Technologies Used in the service Linked Events

  • We use the ID service cookies as part of the content management so that the information about your choices and actions, such as your language settings, will remain consistent across our websites during your visit. The information collected by these cookies is stored in the memory of your computer.
  • Tracking cookies tell about the activity of the user in the service and this data will be stored on a local server where the service Linked Events is also located. The personnel of the city has very restricted access to the information. We use a web analytics application Matomo analytics.

In case you do not want the city to receive the above mentioned information with the cookies, most browsers allow you to disable the cookies by selecting the setting 'Do Not Track' ('Älä Seuraa') of your browser to prevent the web analytics application from using your data.

The Retention Period of the Cookies

By accepting the cookie policy of the service Linked Events, your acceptance will stay active for 90 days after which you will be asked for a new approval in case you return to the service.
